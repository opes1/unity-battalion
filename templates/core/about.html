Unity Battalion Council has served as a beacon of faith, discipline, and Christian character development for boys across our community. Founded on the timeless principles of the Boys' Brigade movement, our battalion has grown from humble beginnings into a thriving council of active companies united by one motto: Sure and Stedfast.

Over the decades, we have commissioned hundreds of officers, enrolled thousands of boys, and watched countless young men grow into leaders of integrity in their homes, churches, and nation. The spirit of the Boys' Brigade, Obedience, Reverence, Discipline, Self-Respect, runs through everything we do.
